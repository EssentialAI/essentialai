The Essential AI
++++++++++++++++++
Welcome to The Essential AI blog.
This section is a series of articles aimed at providing a mathematical intuition behind several Machine Learning algorithms. Many online articles make Machine Learning a plug-and-play game without providing a mathematical comprehension. Articles at The Essential AI are aimed at addressing this issue.
